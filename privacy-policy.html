Privacy Policy for Mayombe

Last updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Mayombe ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name and contact information
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Delivery address
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)

2.2 Location Information

We collect your location data to:

  • Find nearby restaurants
  • Provide accurate delivery estimates
  • Show relevant restaurant options

2.3 Device Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data
  • Crash reports and performance data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfill your food orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our services
  • Send you important updates about your orders
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Restaurant partners to fulfill your orders
  • Delivery drivers to complete deliveries
  • Payment processors to handle transactions
  • Legal authorities when required by law

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
